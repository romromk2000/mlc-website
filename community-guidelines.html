Community Guidelines

Last updated: 28 May 2026  ·  Reviewed every 6 months

My Little Circle is a space built on trust, kindness, and shared experience. Whether you're sharing a park review, finding a local family, or chatting with another parent, these guidelines help keep our community safe and welcoming for every family.

Our Community Values

🤝

Be kind & respectful

Every parent is doing their best. Treat others the way you'd want to be treated.

✅

Be honest

Share genuine experiences and accurate information. Others rely on what you post.

🌍

Be inclusive

Our community welcomes parents of all backgrounds, beliefs, and family structures.

🛡️

Protect children

Child safety is everyone's responsibility. When in doubt, report it.

1. Respectful Behaviour

We have zero tolerance for:

  • Harassment, bullying, or personal attacks on other users
  • Hate speech or discrimination based on race, religion, gender, sexuality, disability, or any other characteristic
  • Threatening or intimidating language
  • Deliberately spreading misinformation about venues, services, or other parents

Disagreements happen — please keep them civil and constructive. If a conversation becomes upsetting, use the in-app Report button rather than escalating.

2. Child Safety

The safety of children is our highest priority.

  • Never share identifying information about children who are not yours (e.g. photos of other people's children without consent)
  • Do not share children's full names, school names, or addresses in public posts or messages
  • Report immediately any content you believe puts a child at risk — we will respond within 24 hours
  • Be cautious when sharing your own child's photo; our app defaults to sharing within your trusted circle only

Any content that exploits, endangers, or sexualises children will result in immediate account termination and referral to the relevant authorities.

3. Reviews & Ratings

Honest reviews help the whole community. Please:

  • Base your review on your genuine personal experience
  • Be specific and constructive — details help other parents more than vague ratings
  • Update your review if things have changed
  • Disclose any affiliation with a venue (e.g. if you work there or received a free visit)

Do not post fake reviews, reviews of venues you have not visited, or reviews intended to harm a competitor. Venue operators may not review their own services.

4. Messaging & Private Chats

Our one-to-one messaging feature is designed to help parents connect safely.

  • Do not solicit personal information (home address, financial details) from other users
  • Do not use messaging to advertise or promote a business without our written permission
  • Do not send unsolicited bulk messages or spam
  • Do not impersonate another person or use a false identity
  • Screenshots from private messages should not be shared publicly without the other person's consent

5. Finding & Meeting Local Families

My Little Circle makes it easy to find parents nearby. Please:

  • Represent yourself and your family honestly in your profile
  • Meet in public places for initial meet-ups with people you don't know
  • Respect other parents' privacy — do not share their details without consent
  • Cancel or update any planned meeting if your plans change

My Little Circle facilitates connections but is not responsible for what happens at in-person meetings. Exercise the same judgement you would when meeting anyone new.

6. Commercial Activity

My Little Circle is a community, not a marketplace.

  • Unsolicited promotions, affiliate links, or MLM content are not permitted
  • Activity providers and venue operators are welcome to claim and manage their listings through our official process — contact partnerships@mylittlecircle.co.uk
  • Sponsored or promotional content must be clearly disclosed

7. Reporting & Moderation

If you see content that violates these guidelines:

  • Use the Report button on any post, review, or profile
  • For urgent child safety concerns, email support@mylittlecircle.co.uk with the subject line URGENT: Child Safety

We review all reports and aim to respond within 24 hours for urgent matters and 5 business days for general reports. Outcomes may include content removal, a warning, temporary suspension, or permanent account ban. We will not share the outcome of reports with the reporting user in order to protect all parties' privacy.

8. Consequences of Violations

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

  • A warning and request to remove content
  • Temporary restriction of messaging or community features
  • Permanent account suspension
  • Referral to law enforcement where required

We apply these consequences at our discretion, taking into account the severity and context of each situation. We always aim to be fair and consistent.

9. A Note on Mental Health

Parenting can be incredibly hard, and we know many of our users are navigating exhaustion, isolation, and anxiety. If you are struggling, please reach out to a healthcare professional or contact the PANDAS Foundation helpline on 0808 1961 776. You are not alone.

If you see a post that suggests someone may be in crisis, please report it immediately and encourage them to seek professional support.
