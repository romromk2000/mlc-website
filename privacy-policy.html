Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 May 2026  ·  KPL Ltd, trading as My Little Circle

We care about protecting your personal data. This policy sets out how any personal data we collect from you, or that you provide to us, will be processed when you use the My Little Circle app (iOS and Android) and website. The My Little Circle Service is operated by KPL Ltd, trading as My Little Circle. For the purpose of the Data Protection Act 2018 and UK GDPR, the data controller is KPL Ltd. Please read this carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

1. Information We May Collect About You

Data you provide to us

We collect and store information you provide when you:

  • Sign up for My Little Circle
  • Save activities or locations in the app
  • Contact us by email or through the app
  • Review an activity, venue, or organiser
  • Provide details about your child (such as age range) to personalise recommendations
  • Enter polls, competitions, or surveys

Interaction and tracking data

We record information about how you use My Little Circle — for example, screens you have viewed, activities you have saved, and searches you have made.

Information collected automatically

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and approximate location (to show nearby venues and activities)
  • Crash reports and performance data to help us fix issues

Information from third parties

  • If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address from that provider
  • Venue and activity data from public sources such as local council websites

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide, or that we record about you, to:

  • Administer the account you sign up for
  • Provide personalised lists of relevant activities and venues based on your child's age and your location
  • Enable community features such as finding local families and private messaging
  • Send you important administrative messages (e.g. significant changes to our terms)
  • Respond to emails and messages you send us
  • Send you marketing messages if you have opted in
  • Improve our products and services by analysing usage data
  • Provide aggregated, anonymised data to our partners
  • Respond to requests by government or law enforcement agencies as required by law

Legal basis for processing

  • Contract: processing necessary to deliver the service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests: improving the app, fraud prevention, and safety
  • Consent: marketing communications and optional features (withdraw at any time)
  • Legal obligation: complying with applicable laws

3. Children's Data

We take the privacy of children very seriously. My Little Circle is intended for parents and carers aged 18 and over — not for direct use by children.

  • We collect limited information about your child (age range) solely to personalise activity recommendations for you as their parent or carer
  • This data is never used for advertising purposes
  • We do not share children's data with third parties except where strictly necessary to deliver the service
  • You may request deletion of your child's data at any time by contacting us at legal@mylittlecircle.co.uk

4. Retention of Your Data

  • We will delete your account data if you have not used My Little Circle for 2 years
  • We will anonymise all interaction data that is older than 12 months
  • Financial and transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years to comply with legal obligations

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time — we will respond within 14 days. You may delete your account from within the app. If you delete your account, we will delete all your account data and anonymise any tracking data connected to it.

5. Transfer of Your Information to Third Parties

We will never sell your personal data. We may share it in the following circumstances:

Companies who provide services to My Little Circle

We use third parties to help us provide our services (e.g. cloud hosting, analytics, customer support). In all cases we will have a suitable data sharing agreement in place to protect your information.

Transfer of assets

If we buy, sell, or transfer any business or assets, or go into insolvency or receivership, we may need to disclose your personal information to the relevant parties.

Legal obligations

If we are under a duty to disclose or share your personal data to comply with any legal obligation, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of My Little Circle, our users, or others.

6. Publicly Accessible Information

If you include personal information in content you post publicly — for example when leaving a review or posting in a community area — it may be possible for other users to identify you or for third parties to collect that information. Please be mindful of what you share in public areas of the app.

7. Location Data

We use your device location (with your permission) to show nearby venues and activities. You can disable location access in your device settings at any time; some features will be limited as a result. We do not store your precise real-time location history.

8. Where We Store Your Data

All information you provide to us is stored on secure servers located in the United Kingdom and other European Economic Area countries. We will take reasonable steps to ensure any transfers of your data outside the EEA are compliant with applicable data protection laws. The transmission of information via the internet is not completely secure — any transmission is at your own risk, and once received we use strict security measures to prevent unauthorised access.

9. Marketing Communications

You may opt in to receive marketing messages from us. You can manage your preferences in your account settings or via the unsubscribe link in any marketing communication.

10. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data in certain circumstances
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we process your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object — object to processing based on our legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, without affecting prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at legal@mylittlecircle.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

11. Changes to This Policy

Any changes we make to this privacy policy will be posted on this page and, where the changes are material, notified to you by email or via in-app notification.

12. Contact Us

Please contact us if you have any questions about this privacy policy.
